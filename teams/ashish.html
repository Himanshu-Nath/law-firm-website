Ashish Prasad

Managing Partner

📍 Location

New Delhi

⚖️ Practice Areas

Strategy & Risk Management, Litigation, Arbitration, Dispute Resolution

Ashish Prasad is the Managing Partner at Acara Law LLP, with over two decades of experience advising clients across a wide range of industries.

His work spans complex areas such as constitutional law, banking, securities, insolvency, infrastructure, environmental law, intellectual property, and dispute resolution.

Known for his sharp legal insight and practical approach, Ashish helps businesses navigate challenging legal situations and reduce risk effectively.

He regularly works with industries including energy, infrastructure, mining, cement, and FMCG, guiding them through regulatory changes, compliance requirements, and high-stakes disputes.

Ashish began his career in 2003 with Lall & Sethi Advocates, where he built strong expertise in intellectual property law, handling trademarks, copyrights, patents, and anti-piracy enforcement across India and South Asia.

Over the years, he has expanded his practice into commercial litigation, arbitration, and strategic advisory, appearing before the Supreme Court of India, various High Courts, and tribunals.

Before founding Acara Law, he was a Partner at Lall & Sethi and Luthra & Luthra, and later served as a Senior Partner at ELP.

Ashish is a graduate of Delhi University and has further specialized in intellectual property law through WIPO. He also holds a Diploma in Cyber Laws from the Indian Law Institute, New Delhi.
